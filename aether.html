Wireless Exposure Assessment

Identify the wireless and RF surface at a site, rate it by technology, and let the instrument write the upgrade. Aether records what you identify and reports it. It does not capture, replay, or transmit anything.

Wireless exposure posture

A field reference for judging the wireless surface by technology. Posture reflects whether the link resists eavesdropping, spoofing, and replay in the published literature. This identifies and rates. It does not reproduce a signal.

TechnologyBandPostureAssessment note
Posture is a starting read from technology type. Keying, firmware, and configuration move the real risk in either direction. Confirm before you write the finding.

Presence is a finding too

Broadcast SSIDs, beacon identifiers, and device names leak the wireless footprint of a site and help an outsider map and target it. Note an oversized broadcast surface as its own observation, separate from the strength of any one link. Reducing what a site announces is often a cheap and useful recommendation.

Tap to Identify

Tap a credential or a nearby device to identify its technology and standard, then send it to Credential for rating. Touchstone reads to identify. It cannot and does not clone, emulate, or dump a credential.

Tap a credential (NFC)

Start the reader, then hold an access card or tag to the top of the phone. Touchstone reports what it can observe and infers the technology family. Web NFC runs on Android Chrome over HTTPS.

Identify a nearby device (Bluetooth)

Pick a nearby Bluetooth device to identify it by name and services. Useful for wireless locks, beacons, and cameras. Best effort, and some devices will not connect.

Manual identifier

No reader on this device, or want to identify from what you can see at the reader. Answer what you know and Touchstone maps it to a likely technology, then sends it to Credential.

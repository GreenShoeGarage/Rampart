Credential technology posture

A field reference for judging an access credential by its technology alone. Posture reflects whether the credential resists copying and replay in the published literature. This identifies and rates. It does not reproduce.

TechnologyBandPostureAssessment note
Posture is a starting read from technology type. Configuration, key management, and reader to panel wiring can move the real risk in either direction. Confirm before you write the finding.

Don't forget the wire

The link between reader and controller matters as much as the card. Legacy Wiegand wiring is unauthenticated and is a known weak point in older installs. OSDP with secure channel is the modern replacement. Treat an unauthenticated reader to panel link as its own finding, independent of the credential.
